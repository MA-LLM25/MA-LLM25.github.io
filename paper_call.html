Call for Papers

The ACM MM 2025 Workshop on Multimedia Analytics with Multimodal Large Language Models invites original research papers and position papers on the following topics:

  • Multimodal Large Language Models
  • Multimedia Analytics
  • Multimedia Interaction
  • Multimedia Summarization
  • Visual Analytics
  • Interactive Multimedia Systems
  • Human-in-the-Loop Reinforcement Learning

Important Dates

  • Paper Submission Deadline: June 13, 2025
  • Author Notification: July 24, 2025
  • Camera-Ready Deadline: August 3, 2025
  • Workshop Date: October 27-28, 2025 (TBA)

Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit full or short papers via the OpenReview submissions portal. Accepted papers will be published in the ACM-MM Workshop Proceedings.

All submissions must follow the ACM Article Template. Full papers can be up to 8 pages, with 2 additional pages for references. Short papers can be up to 4 pages, with 1 additional page for references.
