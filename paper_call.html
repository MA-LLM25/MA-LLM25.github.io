Call for Papers

The First Workshop on Multimedia Analytics with Multimodal Large Language Models at ACM Multimedia 2025 aims to explore the potential and pitfalls of bringing Multimodal Large Language Models into multimedia analytics, and the new forms of interaction between system and experts that emerge from this. To guide this exploration we invite original research and position papers on (but not limited to) the following topics:

  • Multimodal Large Language Models
  • Multimedia Analytics
  • Multimedia Interaction
  • Multimedia Summarisation
  • Visual Analytics
  • Interactive Multimedia Systems
  • Human-in-the-Loop Reinforcement Learning

Important Dates

  • Paper submission: June 13, 2025
  • Author acceptance notification: July 24, 2025
  • Camera-Ready: August 3, 2025
  • Workshop date: TBA (27 Oct - 28 Oct, 2025)

Submission Instructions

Authors are invited to submit original full or short papers to be presented at the workshop upon acceptance. Submissions can be made at the OpenReview submissions portal (this requires a completed OpenReview profile). Accepted papers will be published in the ACM-MM Workshops proceedings.

All submitted papers (.pdf format) must use the ACM Article Template: paper template, matching the main conference. Full papers may consist of up to 8 pages, with up to two additional pages for reference. Short papers may consist of up to 4 pages, with up to one page of references.
